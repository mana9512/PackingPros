How It Works

Registration as User or a Partner

Register on ReadyToMove website as a user or a partner. As a user,you will get to choose from the variant services and as a partner, you can place bid the client requirements.

Choose the Service

As a user,you can select from the three main services provided by ReadyToMove- packers & movers based on the type of requirement you have.

Select the Date & Time

Once you have chosen the services, the next thing you have to do is select the pickup and delivery date and timing as well as the place so as to let the partners know about your exact requirements.By knowing the specific date and timings, the transporters and packing & moving services will be able to place a bid on your requirement in an effectual manner.

Place Bids on the Requirements

Being a Partner, you can place bids on the customer requirements based on the pickup and delivery times as well as the location. You will be able to view the lowest bid on a particular requirement as well.
